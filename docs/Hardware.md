# Hardware 硬件

## Technical specifications  技术规格

The console's main central processing unit (CPU) was produced by Ricoh, which manufactured different versions for NTSC and PAL regions; NTSC consoles have a 2A03 clocked at 1.79 MHzTooltip megahertz, and PAL consoles have a 2A07 clocked at 1.66 MHz. Both CPUs are unlicensed variants of the MOS Technology 6502, an 8-bit microprocessor prevalent in contemporary home computers and consoles. Nintendo ostensibly disabled the 6502's binary-coded decimal mode on them to avoid patent infringement against or licensing fees towards MOS Technology, which was owned by then-rival Commodore International. The CPU has access to 2 KBTooltip kilobyte of onboard work RAMTooltip random-access memory.

该游戏机的主中央处理器 (CPU) 由理光公司生产，该公司为 NTSC 和 PAL 地区生产了不同版本；NTSC 游戏机配备主频为 1.79 MHz（兆赫兹）的 2A03 芯片，而 PAL 游戏机配备主频为 1.66 MHz 的 2A07 芯片。这两款 CPU 都是 MOS Technology 6502 的非授权版本，6502 是一款 8 位微处理器，在当时的家用电脑和游戏机中非常普遍。任天堂表面上禁用了 6502 的二进制编码十进制模式，以避免侵犯 MOS Technology 的专利或向其支付许可费，而 MOS Technology 当时是其竞争对手 Commodore International 旗下的公司。该 CPU 可访问 2 KB（千字节）的板载工作随机存取存储器 (RAM)。

The console's graphics are handled by a Ricoh 2C02, a processor known as the Picture Processing Unit (PPU) that is clocked at 5.37 MHz. A derivative of the Texas Instruments TMS9918 (a video display controller used in the ColecoVision), the PPU features 2 KB of video RAM, 256 bytes of on-die "object attribute memory" (OAM) to store sprite display information on up to 64 sprites, and 28 bytes of RAM to store information on the YIQ-based color palette; the console can display up to 25 colors simultaneously out of 54 usable colors.

该游戏机的图形处理由 Ricoh 2C02 芯片负责，这是一款被称为图像处理单元（PPU）的处理器，时钟频率为 5.37 MHz。PPU 是德州仪器 TMS9918（用于 ColecoVision 游戏机的视频显示控制器）的衍生产品，它配备 2 KB 显存、256 字节的片上“对象属性存储器”（OAM），用于存储最多 64 个精灵的显示信息，以及 28 字节的 RAM，用于存储基于 YIQ 色彩空间的调色板信息；该游戏机可以同时显示 54 种可用颜色中的最多 25 种颜色。

The console's standard display resolution is 256 × 240 pixels, though video output options vary between models. The original Famicom features only radio frequency (RF) modulator output, and the NES additionally supports composite video via RCA connectors. The redesigned Famicom omits the RF modulator entirely, only outputting composite video via a proprietary "multi-out" connector first introduced on the Super Famicom/SNES; conversely, the redesigned NES features RF modulator output only, though a version of the model including the "multi-out" connector was produced in rare quantities.

该游戏机的标准显示分辨率为 256 × 240 像素，但不同型号的视频输出选项有所不同。初代 Famicom 仅支持射频 (RF) 调制器输出，而 NES 则额外支持通过 RCA 连接器输出复合视频。重新设计的 Famicom 完全取消了射频调制器，仅通过 Super Famicom/SNES 上首次引入的专用“多功能输出”接口输出复合视频；相反，重新设计的 NES 仅支持射频调制器输出，不过也曾少量生产过包含“多功能输出”接口的版本。

The console produces sound via an audio processing unit (APU) integrated into the processor. It supports a total of five sound channels: two pulse wave channels, one triangle wave channel, one white noise channel, and one DPCMTooltip differential pulse-code modulation channel for sample playback. Audio playback speed is dependent on the CPU clock rate, which is set by a crystal oscillator.

该游戏机通过集成在处理器中的音频处理单元（APU）发出声音。它总共支持五个声音通道：两个脉冲波通道、一个三角波通道、一个白噪声通道和一个用于样本播放的DPCM（差分脉冲编码调制）通道。音频播放速度取决于CPU时钟频率，而时钟频率由晶体振荡器设定。
